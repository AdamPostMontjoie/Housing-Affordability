about the data
the data for the real median household income was taken from the Federal Reserve Economic Data distributed by the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis
the data for the housing prices was taken from the zillow home value index
How is affordability calculated?
The old school recommendation is that a house costs between 3 to 5 times your yearly income. 
to calculate the affordability at a time, i compared the home value in an area with the median household income
